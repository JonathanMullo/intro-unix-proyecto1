# Section 4: Privacy Reflection

Privacy in the digital world is extremely important because a large amount of personal information is stored online.

Using tools such as encryption, secure passwords, and hashing helps protect information from unauthorized access.

Users should follow good security practices, such as:
- Not reusing passwords
- Using strong passwords
- Enabling two-factor authentication

## Conclusion

Cybersecurity is essential to protect personal data and maintain privacy in today's digital environment.
