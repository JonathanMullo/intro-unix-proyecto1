# Section 3: Hash Results

## Hash Comparison

| Algorithm | Length  | Security Level |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| MD5      | 128-bit | Low           |
| SHA1     | 160-bit | Medium        |
| SHA256   | 256-bit | High          |

## Results

- MD5 is fast but insecure.
- SHA1 has known vulnerabilities.
- SHA256 is more secure and recommended.

## Hashcat Usage

Hashcat was used to attempt password cracking.

Results:
- Weak passwords were cracked quickly.
- Strong passwords could not be cracked.
